Drawnar.io

About:

Drawnario is also one of my favorite projects, cause it was just an big idea at the beginning, but then became reality. It was developed within 1 month, and i came through a lot of difficulties coding it.

General Idea:

There is a canvas that allows you to draw whatever you want with 2 different-sized grids. Also, there are 15 different colors and 4 unique operations that help you visualize a lot of drawing ideas. Come up with an idea, draw it and then save it to your local files so as not to lose it, or to continue later. The game supports modifing your drawnings or repainting them and saving them again, and deleting your files.

Operations:

  • Undo

    • This operation cancel your most recent change at the grid.

  • Erase

    • Press erase and then click at blocks of the grid that you want to erase.

  • Fill

    • With fill operation, you fill the entire canvas with the selected color.

  • Reset

    • This operation clean ups the whole canvas, by turning it into total white.
